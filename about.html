What's My Y?

"Two things drive me: The research rabbit hole, and the moment I step in front of the camera."

One sharpens my thinking. The other brings it to life. And when a brand sees the final output and feels exactly what I intended... that is the moment I work for.

"Tell me the idea. I'll tell you how it looks when it's done."

That's genuinely how I work. You bring the brief, the product, the objective. I get into the brand's world, the competitor landscape, the consumer's head and come back with a vision so clear you can almost see it before we've shot a single frame.

It didn't start in content though. I spent a year in HR, hiring across industries, understanding what actually drives people. Not professionally, but humanly. What makes them say yes. What makes them feel seen. That lens never left me. It just found a better home in storytelling.

Then a year at LIT School going deep into strategy, scripting and creation. And somewhere between the research decks and the shoot days, I figured out exactly what I'm good at. Taking a brand's raw idea and engineering it into something people actually feel.

I work best with brands that are building something they believe in. Because that energy shows up in the work every single time.
